15 Years of Excellence

I have been dedicated to Indian and Nepalese cuisine in Hiroshima for 15 years, seeking flavors that can only be found here. After the pandemic, my commitment to providing healing and wholesome meals has grown even stronger.

We accommodate Halal and Vegetarian diets with advance notice. Please feel free to consult our staff.

The Ayurvedic Approach

Originating in India, Ayurveda emphasizes the body's natural healing power and achieving balance through food. At Namaste, we use over a dozen spices and diverse vegetables to create healthy, blissful dishes for our guests.
