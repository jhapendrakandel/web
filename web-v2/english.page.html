Our Story

15 Years of Passion for Indian Cuisine

With 15 years of experience in Indian and Nepalese cuisine in Hiroshima, we have pursued unique flavours you can only find here — now operating across three locations.

Our restaurant focuses on Ayurvedic cuisine, hoping to heal tired bodies and nurture self-healing abilities. With prior notice, we can accommodate halal and vegetarian requests.

Our Philosophy

Challenging Ayurveda

Originating in India, Ayurveda emphasises the body's natural healing abilities and restoring balance through diet. At Namaste, we contribute to our customers' health through dishes made with dozens of spices and diverse vegetables.

Our menu contains eggs, dairy, wheat, soy, salad oil, cashews, coconut, and tomatoes. Customers with allergies should take caution — please ask our staff for details.
